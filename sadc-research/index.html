SPACECRAFT ATTITUDE MODELING RESEARCH PROJECT

Matthew Robinaugh

Mar 15, 2023 • 1 min read

The Spacecraft Attitude Control Implementation and Development project was done under the guidance of Dr. Bradley Wall and funded by the ERAU Undergraduate Research Institute and Arizona Space Grant. The goal of the project was to develop a platform and the control law to enable simulated one-dimensional attitude control of a physical model. I was the sole researcher on the team and was in charge of developing the control law as well as designing and testing the platform. This project helped teach me how to apply the theory we learned in class to a real-world system with all of its imperfections.
