Projects

My senior design project is to complete a physical model to demonstrate the capability of a propulsive rocket landing using a solid rocket motor. My team of 6 engineering students and I are currently in the final build and test phase of the project. For further details please follow the link.

I was part of an Urban Aerial Mobility (UAM) research project in collaboration between Embry-Riddle and Honeywell Aerospace. The goal of the project was to build and test a model of a UAM to characterize its flight performance for steady flight conditions as well as during ascent and descent. For further details please follow the link.

Under the supervision of Dr. Bradley Wall, I spent a semester developing a physical of a satellite to conduct testing and implementation on one-dimensional attitude control laws. The control law was developed through Simulink and the physical implementation was done through an Arduino, a reaction wheel, and a 3-D printed housing to contain the electronics and other components. For for further detail please follow the link.
