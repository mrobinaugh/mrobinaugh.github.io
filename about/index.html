Hi I'm Matthew

Welcome to my digital portfolio! I am a Control Dynamics Engineer at United Launch Alliance in Centennial, CO. My job primarily involves the design of mission-specific autopilots, stability analysis, and internal tool development for our team. In my free time, I love staying active and building things. Whether that is coding up a project, using my 3D printer to create random gadgets, or tinkering with my computer. I made this website to document some of the more interesting projects I've been a part of for the last couple of years. If you like to get an overview of some of these projects, you can visit the link here.

I discovered my passion for aerospace and software development in high school working through my engineering classes and tackling complex problems on my robotics team. This drove me to study Aerospace Engineering in college to learn as much as I could of both. Even after graduating, I am still trying to learn as much as I can every day.
